# Growth Assets

## Five Launch Posts

1. Reddit / Indie Hackers

I built a tiny browser tool for creators who sell on Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Ko-fi, Etsy, or Stripe and then get stuck cleaning order CSVs. It normalizes headers, emails, dates, amounts, removes refunded/failed rows, dedupes buyers, and exports a clean customer list. Free preview handles the first 25 rows; full export is a one-time unlock. I am looking for messy sample headers to improve presets.

2. Product Hunt

CreatorCSV Cleaner turns messy creator-platform order exports into clean, import-ready customer lists. Upload or paste a CSV, choose a platform preset, remove duplicates/refunds, preview results, and export a normalized CSV. Built for indie sellers after launches when spreadsheets are annoying and time is short.

3. Reddit creator community

If you sell digital products, how do you clean buyer CSVs before importing them into your email tool? I made a simple browser-side cleaner for Gumroad/Lemon/Ko-fi/Etsy/Stripe exports. It does not ask for platform login and does not upload your CSV to a server. Feedback welcome, especially weird header formats.

4. Indie Hackers build-in-public

Shipping a deliberately small paid tool: CreatorCSV Cleaner. Narrow promise, no AI dependency, no login, no scraping. Free users can clean/export 25 rows; paid users unlock full export. The first experiment is whether creators will pay $9 to remove launch-day spreadsheet cleanup.

5. Product-led support post

I wrote a short guide on cleaning order exports before customer imports. Common fixes: lowercase emails, normalize amounts, remove refunds/failed payments, dedupe by email/order/date, and keep platform/source columns. I bundled those steps into a browser tool if anyone wants to test it.
