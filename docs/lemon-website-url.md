# Lemon Squeezy Website URL

Lemon Squeezy may ask for a website URL during store setup or review. Use a public product website URL, not:

- `http://127.0.0.1:3000`
- a private preview link that requires login
- a fake domain
- a Lemon product checkout link if the form specifically asks for your product website

## Best Free Option

Deploy this Next.js app to Vercel and use the free Vercel subdomain:

`https://creatorcsv-cleaner.vercel.app`

The exact subdomain may differ if that name is taken.

## What The Site Now Includes

- Product landing page
- Working free CSV preview
- Pricing
- Checkout page
- Privacy policy
- Terms of use
- Refund policy
- Contact email placeholder

## What To Send Back To Codex

After deployment, send only:

- the public website URL
- the public support email
- the Lemon checkout link after Lemon approves or lets you create products

Do not send passwords, verification codes, identity documents, or payment account credentials.
